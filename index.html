🛠️ Project

This portfolio depicts our smart city design project. Team members will identify business case studies and incorporate appropriate algorithms to solve problems and develop the city design.

📋 Problem Definition

The government of India wants to convert a 1500 square kilometer area into a smart city. They seek a city plan proposal, considering the failures of past cities like Lavasa and Masdar.

  • Nandita M.G: Tourism, Energy Generation, Road Transport
