The course project we are working on, aims to design a city that is sustainable and can host all the required facilities that contributes towards the development of the city along with preserving the intricate culture of the city , state and country through tourism.

ğŸ“‹ CHITRANAGARI : The City of Art

The designed city is assumed to be situated in the state of Rajasthan, India, with area of approximately 1423 square km area. The city is called Chitranagari which translates to 'City of Art'. Just like it's name , the city offers it's visitors immense cultural heritage and diversity in the form of museum, fort and palace. It also hosts recreational sites such as amusement park, zoological and botanical garden. The city is well connected with roads and higways on the outskirts , supported by metro within the city.There are two government offices(North and South hub) for smooth working. These along with other details make this city a sustainable and one of major economically driving cities.
